MT INSPECTION

Magnetic Particle Testing

At INTERNATIONAL INSTITUTE OF PAUT NDT, we provide professional Magnetic Particle Testing (MT) services to detect surface and near-surface discontinuities in ferromagnetic materials.

Our MT Services Include:

Surface Crack Detection:
Identifying cracks, seams, laps, and other surface defects in ferromagnetic materials.

Weld Inspection:
Detecting weld discontinuities and ensuring weld quality and structural reliability.

Inspection of Castings and Forgings:
Examining manufactured components for production-related defects.

In-Service Component Inspection:
Assessing machinery and structural parts for fatigue cracks and service-related damage.

Wet and Dry MT Techniques:
Providing both wet fluorescent and dry powder magnetic particle inspection methods.
