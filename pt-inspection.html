PT INSPECTION

Liquid Penetrant Testing

At INTERNATIONAL INSTITUTE OF PAUT NDT, we provide high-quality Liquid Penetrant Testing (PT) services for detecting surface-breaking defects in non-porous materials.

Our PT Services Include:

Surface Crack Detection:
Detecting fine cracks, porosity, laps, and leaks on material surfaces.

Weld Inspection:
Ensuring weld quality by identifying surface discontinuities.

Casting and Forging Inspection:
Examining manufactured components for production-related defects.

Leak Testing Applications:
Identifying leakage paths in critical components and assemblies.

Visible and Fluorescent PT Methods:
Providing both visible dye penetrant and fluorescent penetrant inspection techniques.
