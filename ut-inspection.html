UT INSPECTION

Ultrasonic Testing

At INTERNATIONAL INSTITUTE OF PAUT NDT, we offer advanced Ultrasonic Testing (UT) services for detecting internal flaws and measuring material thickness with high accuracy.

Our UT Services Include:

Internal Flaw Detection:
Identifying cracks, voids, inclusions, laminations, and discontinuities within materials.

Thickness Measurement:
Measuring wall thickness in pipes, vessels, tanks, and structural components.

Weld Inspection:
Evaluating weld quality and detecting internal weld defects.

Corrosion Monitoring:
Assessing material loss due to corrosion or erosion in industrial equipment.

Forging and Casting Inspection:
Ensuring the integrity and quality of critical manufactured components.
