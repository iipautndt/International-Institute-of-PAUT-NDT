Eddy Current Testing

At INTERNATIONAL INSTITUTE OF PAUT NDT, we provide Eddy Current Testing (ET) services to ensure the integrity and reliability of critical assets.

Our ET Services Include:

Surface Crack Detection:
Identifying cracks, pits, and corrosion on conductive materials.

Tube & Pipe Inspection:
Inspecting heat exchanger tubes, boiler tubes, and pipelines for defects.

Coating Thickness Measurement:
Measuring non-conductive coating thickness on conductive surfaces.

Material Sorting:
Identifying material grades, conductivity, and heat treatment conditions.

Weld Inspection:
Detecting weld defects and evaluating weld integrity.
